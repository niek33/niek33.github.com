On Writing Well

Aug 19, 2018 8:10· 69 words · 1 minutes read

Writing well is a key skill paying dividends in many areas of life.

  • Keep things simple. Get rid of any unnecessary words to get along the message.
  • The first line is aimed to grab the reader immediately.
  • Write short sentences.
  • Avoid putting multiple thoughts into one sentence.
  • Put object before action. The boy hit the ball vs. The ball was hit by the boy.

Hat tip to Scott Adams.
