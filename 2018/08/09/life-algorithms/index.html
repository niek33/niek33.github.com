Life Algorithms

Aug 09, 2018 5:39· 134 words · 1 minutes read

Over time I gathered some insight/habits/strategies that I found beneficial. This living document collects them all.

  • Prioritize sleep. Aim at 8+ hours every night. Avoid/limit caffeine after noon, alcohol after 6pm.
  • Practice GTD. Keep at it. Monitor which process bottlenecks when stuck, avoid tool optimization.
  • Pomodoro, amico mio.
  • Meditate.
  • Anki is your best friend for learning and knowledge retention.
  • Combat procrastination by increasing expectancy, value,optimism. Reduce impulsiveness.
  • Draft high level goals, subdivide them in projects and track them properly.
  • Read books as if you study them. Annotate, summarize and feed them to Anki.
  • Write daily.
  • Live on a budget. Get familiar with envelope budgeting, use it for all spending decisions. Also Buckets.
  • Lift weights. It helps boost physical and mental health.
  • Use the concept of transaction costs to your advantage.
